About

I’m a software engineer with a background in music performance, engineering, and education. Until the coronavirus pandemic hit I followed the calls and opportunities that kept jumping in front of me going from a degree in commercial music, to touring the world, to teaching jazz at a top 10 public university while earning a masters degree in music performance, to living and working in NYC. I’ve always enjoyed coding and when the global music industry hit pause I jumped at the opportunity to hit play on a new career in software engineering.
